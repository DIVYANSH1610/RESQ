Our Mission — RESQ is committed to providing rapid emergency response, medical assistance, volunteer coordination, and emotional support to individuals affected by natural disasters or crises — with speed, compassion, and trust.

Core Services

What We Provide

🏥
Medical Aid

On-site emergency treatment, mobile clinics, and first-aid supply distribution to affected communities.

🚁
Rescue Operations

Deployment of trained personnel during floods, earthquakes, fires, and other critical disaster scenarios.

🤝
Counseling & Relief

Psychological support, food distribution, and temporary shelter setup for displaced civilians.

📍
Quell Zones

Verified safe gathering points mapped with live volunteer presence, capacity, and supplies available.

📡
Live Coordination

Real-time communication between volunteer teams, emergency services, and people in distress.

🎓
Training & Skills

Free first-aid, evacuation planning, and crisis communication training for registered volunteers.
