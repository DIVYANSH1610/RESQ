RESQ Services

Respond. Evacuate. Support. Quickly.

Our Mission

RESQ is committed to providing rapid emergency response, medical assistance, and emotional support to individuals affected by natural disasters or crises.

What We Do

  • Medical Aid: On-site emergency treatment, mobile clinics, and first-aid supply distribution.
  • Rescue Operations: Deployment of trained personnel during floods, earthquakes, and fires.
  • Counseling & Relief: Psychological support, food distribution, and temporary shelter setup.

Donation & Relief Efforts

Your donations directly fund emergency supplies, shelter, food, and first-aid kits for disaster victims.
