About RESQ

Community-Driven
Disaster Response

RESQ is a real-time disaster response platform that connects people in crisis with trained volunteers, emergency services, and verified safe zones — the moment they need it most. We believe the fastest responder is often the one already in the community.

Our Mission — To reduce chaos, isolation, and misinformation during disasters by building a trusted network of volunteers and civilians who respond together with speed, compassion, and coordination.
