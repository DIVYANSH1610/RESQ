About RESQ

RESQ is a real-time disaster response and evacuation platform designed to help civilians during emergencies by providing intelligent evacuation routes, rapid SOS assistance, and a live volunteer network. Powered by Groq AI and Fluvio streaming, it ensures users receive timely help, accurate updates, and a reliable connection to safety.

Our Mission

We aim to reduce chaos, misinformation, and isolation during disasters. By combining real-time AI and live data streaming, RESQ bridges the gap between victims and responders with speed, clarity, and trust.

Core Features

AutoQ: Intelligent routing to safe zones.

Q-Link: Live connection between users and volunteers.

SOS Detection: Fast voice/text alerts analyzed with AI.

Quell Zones: Dynamic safe areas with volunteer presence.

Live Tracking: Real-time updates and location sharing.

Powered By

Groq AI: For instant voice-to-text SOS and emotion detection.
